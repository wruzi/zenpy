Rules & Guidelines

Strict Moderation

ZenPy has zero tolerance for cheating, harassment, abuse, spam, and malicious behavior. Violations can trigger warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity and repetition.

Chat & Community

  • No hate speech, personal attacks, threats, or bullying.
  • No NSFW content, slurs, discriminatory language, or harassment.
  • No spam, flood messages, repeated self-promo, or fake giveaways.
  • No malicious or phishing links, malware, or exploit sharing.

Learning Integrity

  • No plagiarism, automation scripts, answer bots, or bypass tools.
  • No abusing copy-paste loopholes or platform vulnerabilities.
  • No multi-account farming for XP, Zen, or leaderboard manipulation.
  • Any attempt to tamper with score/progress data is a high-risk violation.

Ban Triggers

  • Repeated abuse reports with verified evidence.
  • Malicious behavior affecting users or platform security.
  • Cheat patterns, tamper attempts, or suspicious automation.
  • Evading a previous ban by creating alternate accounts.

Penalty Matrix
